# ASIL Safety Ratings

ASIL (Automotive Safety Integrity Level) classifies safety requirements per ISO 26262.

## Levels

- ASIL A (lowest)
- ASIL B
- ASIL C
- ASIL D (highest)

## Application

Battery management functions are typically rated ASIL C or D due to [thermal runaway](thermal-runaway.md) risk.
