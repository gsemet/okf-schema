# Thermal Modeling

Thermal models predict temperature distribution across battery packs.

## Approaches

- Lumped parameter models
- CFD simulations
- Data-driven models

## Applications

Used to design cooling strategies and predict [thermal runaway](../safety/thermal-runaway.md) risk.
