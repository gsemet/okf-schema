# Overview

The cooling system removes excess heat from the battery pack to keep
cells within their optimal operating temperature range, typically
15–35 degrees Celsius.

# Cooling Technologies

| Technology | Description | Use Case |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| Air cooling | Forced convection over fins | Low-cost, low-power packs |
| Liquid cooling | Coolant channels in cold plates | High-performance EVs |
| Phase-change material | Absorbs heat via solid-liquid transition | Passive thermal buffering |
| Refrigerant direct | Direct refrigerant evaporation | Ultra-fast charging |
