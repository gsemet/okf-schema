# Overview

The lithium-ion cell is the basic building block of an EV battery pack.
It stores electrical energy through reversible lithium-ion intercalation
between the anode and cathode.

# Key Parameters

| Parameter | Typical Value | Description |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Nominal voltage | 3.6–3.7 V | Operating voltage under normal conditions |
| Capacity | 50–100 Ah | Charge storage in ampere-hours |
| Energy density | 250–300 Wh/kg | Gravimetric energy density |
