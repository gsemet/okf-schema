# Overview

Cell chemistry defines the anode, cathode, and electrolyte materials
used in a lithium-ion cell. The choice of chemistry directly impacts
energy density, cycle life, safety, and cost.

# Common Chemistries

| Chemistry | Cathode | Anode | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|-------|
| NMC | LiNiMnCoO2 | Graphite | High energy density, common in EVs |
| LFP | LiFePO4 | Graphite | Safer, longer cycle life, lower energy density |
| NCA | LiNiCoAlO2 | Graphite | Used by Tesla, high energy density |
